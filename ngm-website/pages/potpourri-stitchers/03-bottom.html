What we explore

A little of everything

We gather the 3rd Tuesday of each month, 10 am to 2 pm, in person in a member's home — meetings rotate between members' homes, and the host's address is shared with the group each month. If you like to wander across techniques, this is your group. Potpourri Stitchers explore a wide range of hand-work — surface embroidery one month, heirloom sewing or quilting the next — in the relaxed setting of a member's home.

  • Embroidery
  • Stumpwork
  • Shadow work
  • Heirloom sewing
  • Smocking
  • Quilting

Good to know

Common questions

Where does the group meet?
In members' homes, rotating each month. The host's address is shared with the group.
What should I bring?
Bring your own lunch. The hostess provides coffee, tea, and dessert. Please RSVP to the hostess so she can plan.
What do you work on?
A variety — embroidery, stumpwork, shadow work, heirloom sewing, smocking, and quilting. Bring whatever you're stitching.
What skill level do I need?
Any — all are welcome.
