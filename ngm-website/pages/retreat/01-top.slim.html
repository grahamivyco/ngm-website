About the retreat

More than a meeting

Once a year the Guild gathers for a retreat — a relaxed few days to take a class from a visiting teacher, dig into a project, and enjoy the company of fellow stitchers. It's one of the most anticipated events on the calendar.

The details

What to expect

When

August 12–15, 2026 (Wednesday–Saturday). Classes begin at 9 a.m.

Where

Crowne Plaza Minneapolis West, 3131 Campus Drive, Plymouth, MN 55441.

Registration fee

$555 — covers the participation fee and 11 meals (four breakfasts, four lunches, three suppers). Class kit fees are extra and vary by class (about $75–$115). Pay by PayPal or check, full or in two payments.

Stay over

Optional — the Crowne Plaza group rate is $99 per night plus tax. Book by July 20 to get the rate.

Register by

Registration is open now. Class deadlines: Cissy Bailey Smith by May 15, Kim Sanders by June 5, and Stephanie Burnham and Independent Study by July 15.

Cancellation

Cancel by June 10 for a full refund (minus a $25 processing fee), or by July 16 for a half refund. Requests must be made in writing.

Independent study

Classes are optional — you can register purely for independent study and stitch your own projects, with two large rooms of clustered tables just for that.
