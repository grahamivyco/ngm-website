Good to know

Common questions

When and where do the Stitch-Ins meet?
Both meet the 4th Tuesday of the month. The Daytime Stitch-In runs 10 AM to 2 PM and is hybrid — in person at MN Valley Unitarian Universalist Fellowship in Bloomington, or by video. The Evening Stitch-In runs 7 to 9 PM by videoconference only.
Do I have to follow the program?
No. There's a monthly program, but you're always welcome to bring your own project and stitch along with the group instead.
What skill level do I need?
Any. All skill levels are welcome, from curious beginners to experienced stitchers.
How do I get the video link?
The videoconference link is on the Member Resources page — log in to access it. Trouble connecting? Email inquiry@needleworkguildmn.org.
