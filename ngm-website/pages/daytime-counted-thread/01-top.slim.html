What we explore

Counted techniques, every month

Each meeting centers on a program or demonstration, then time to stitch and share. Whether you love the precision of counted thread or the texture of canvas needlepoint, there's something here — and you're always welcome to bring your own project.

  • Cross stitch
  • Hardanger
  • Pulled thread
  • Drawn thread
  • Blackwork
  • Hemstitching
  • Canvas needlepoint
