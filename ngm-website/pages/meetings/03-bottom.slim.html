Our groups

Find your people

Stitch-Ins welcome any technique and skill level. Special Interest Groups focus on a particular discipline. All members are welcome at any group, any month — attend as many as you like at no extra cost, and you're always free to work on your own project, whatever the group's focus.

Daytime meetings

Stitch-In

Daytime Stitch-In

4th Tuesday · 10 am – 2 pm

An open stitching session with a monthly program — presentations, technique demos, and projects. All needlework welcome. You're always free to work on your own project.

Hybrid
Special Interest Group

Daytime Counted Thread & Needlepoint

2nd Tuesday · 10 am – 2 pm

Explores counted techniques — cross stitch, hardanger, pulled thread, drawn thread, blackwork, hemstitching, and canvas needlepoint. Programs alternate monthly between counted thread and needlepoint.

Hybrid
Special Interest Group

Potpourri Stitchers

3rd Tuesday · 10 am – 2 pm

A variety group exploring embroidery, stumpwork, shadow work, heirloom sewing, smocking, and quilting. Meets in members' homes. Bring your own lunch — the hostess provides coffee, tea, and dessert. Please RSVP to the hostess.

In person

Evening meetings

Stitch-In

Evening Stitch-In

4th Tuesday · 7 – 9 pm

Same warm spirit as the daytime session — great for members who work during the day. Programs may differ from the daytime meeting.

Virtual only
Special Interest Group

Evening Needlepointers

3rd Thursday · 7 – 9 pm

Explores specialty threads and stitches on canvas, including counted needlepoint and finishing techniques. All stitchers welcome regardless of experience level.

Come & Stitch

A relaxed, social stitching session open to everyone — members and non-members alike. Bring any needlework project. No programs, no pressure, just community.
