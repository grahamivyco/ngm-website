Good to know

Common questions

Where does the group meet?
In members' homes, rotating each month. The host's address is shared with the group.
What should I bring?
Bring your own lunch. The hostess provides coffee, tea, and dessert. Please RSVP to the hostess so she can plan.
What do you work on?
A variety — embroidery, stumpwork, shadow work, heirloom sewing, smocking, and quilting. Bring whatever you're stitching.
What skill level do I need?
Any — all are welcome.
