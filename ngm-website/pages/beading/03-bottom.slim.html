Beadwork, when it sparks

Beading runs as occasional, event-based sessions rather than a fixed monthly meeting — there's no set schedule. When a session is scheduled it's announced on the calendar, and it runs hybrid: in person or by video. Beading projects are always welcome at any Stitch-In in the meantime.

Common questions

When does beading meet?
There's no fixed monthly schedule — beading runs as occasional, event-based sessions. Watch the calendar for the next one.
Can I do beadwork with the Guild in the meantime?
Yes — bring a beading project to any Stitch-In and stitch alongside the group.
How do I get involved?
Contact Marilyn McMahon to express interest, or watch the calendar for scheduled sessions.
Is it in person or online?
Hybrid when a session runs — join in person or by video.
