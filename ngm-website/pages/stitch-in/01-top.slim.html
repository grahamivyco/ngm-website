Monthly meetings

Stitch-Ins

Two open stitching sessions each month, with exposure to a wide range of fiber arts through lectures, presentations, and hands-on projects. Needleworkers of any skill level are welcome.

Don't feel like following the program? You're always welcome to join and work on your own project.

Two sessions

Day or evening

Both Stitch-Ins meet on the 4th Tuesday of the month. Programs may differ between the two, and you're welcome to attend either or both.

Hybrid

Daytime Stitch-In

4th Tuesday · 10 AM – 2 PM

A daytime session with a monthly program — presentations, demos, and projects spanning a wide range of fiber arts. Work along with the program or bring your own project.

  • Hybrid: in person or by video
  • In person at MVUUF, Bloomington
  • All skill levels welcome
Virtual only

Evening Stitch-In

4th Tuesday · 7 – 9 PM

The same warm, social spirit in an evening session — great for members who work during the day. Programs may differ from the daytime meeting.

  • By videoconference only
  • Link on the Member Resources page
  • All skill levels welcome
