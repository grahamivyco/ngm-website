What we explore

A little of everything

If you like to wander across techniques, this is your group. Potpourri Stitchers explore a wide range of hand-work — surface embroidery one month, heirloom sewing or quilting the next — in the relaxed setting of a member's home.

  • Embroidery
  • Stumpwork
  • Shadow work
  • Heirloom sewing
  • Smocking
  • Quilting
