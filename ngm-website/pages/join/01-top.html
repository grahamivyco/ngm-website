Why join

More than a membership

Whatever your skill level — from curious beginner to lifelong stitcher — you're welcome here. Connect with people who share your passion, sharpen your craft, and give back to a community that's been stitching together since 1972.

Lasting friendships

Make rewarding connections with people who share your passion, and enjoy needlework as both a pastime and an art form.

Grow your skills

Expand your knowledge and try new techniques across our many monthly meetings — attend as many as you like.

Lead and give back

Volunteer your talents — serve on the board, edit the newsletter, organize classes, or help plan the annual Retreat.

What you get

Your annual membership includes

The Needle's Eye newsletter

Our bi-monthly newsletter with updates on upcoming programs and activities.

All monthly meetings

Participate in any or all of the Guild's monthly Stitch-Ins and Special Interest Groups.

Educational materials

Access to program materials prepared by fellow members for monthly meetings.

Workshops with pro instructors

Enroll in workshops covering a diverse array of techniques for all skill levels.

The circulating library

Borrow from a growing collection of books, periodicals, and DVDs.

New to the Guild? Anyone with an interest in needlework is welcome — no experience needed — and your first visit to any meeting is always free. Come & Stitch and Sunday Tea & Stitch are open to everyone with no membership required. Ready to join?
