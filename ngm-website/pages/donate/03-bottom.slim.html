Other ways to give

Prefer to give another way?

The form above is the quickest way to give online. If you'd rather send a check, dedicate a gift, or give in someone's honor, here's how.

By mail

Send a check to Needlework Guild of Minnesota, PO Box 16506, St. Louis Park, MN 55416.

In honor or memory

Dedicate a gift to a stitcher you love — just note the dedication with your donation.

Monthly giving

Set up a recurring monthly donation in the form above to support the Guild all year long.

Tax-deductible

The Guild is a registered 501(c)(3); gifts are tax-deductible as allowed by law.

Good to know

Common questions

Is my donation tax-deductible?
Yes — the Guild is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit, so gifts are tax-deductible as allowed by law.
Can I give monthly?
Yes. Online donations can be set up as a one-time gift or a recurring monthly donation.
Where does my gift go?
To programs and guest instructors, the members' library, community outreach, and keeping dues affordable.
Can I give in someone's honor?
Absolutely — note the honoree with your gift and we'll record the dedication.
Thank you

Every gift counts

However you give, your support keeps the needle arts alive in Minnesota. Thank you for being part of it.
