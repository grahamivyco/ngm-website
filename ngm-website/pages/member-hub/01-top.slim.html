Get involved

Volunteer positions

The Guild runs entirely on member volunteers. These are the roles that keep it going — step into one for the year, or help out however you can.

President

Leads the board, sets the year’s direction, and runs the annual meeting.

Vice President

Supports the President, steps in when needed, and leads special projects.

Treasurer

Keeps the books — dues, expenses, deposits, and the annual budget.

Secretary

Takes minutes at meetings and keeps the Guild’s official records.

Events & Programs

Plans the year’s programs and guest speakers and books instructors.

Sunday Blast Editor

Writes and sends the weekly member email of reminders and news.

Advertising

Coordinates advertisers and sponsor listings, and spreads the word.

Website Manager

Keeps the website current — pages, events, and member info.

Website Communications

Gathers Guild news and turns it into content for the website.

Librarian

Cares for the lending library — checking books in and out.

Shop Manager

Runs the Guild shop — threads, kits, and supplies at meetings.

Retreat Chair

Organizes the annual retreat from start to finish, usually as a team.

Daytime Stitch-In Leader

Hosts the daytime Stitch-In — welcoming members and opening the space.

Evening Stitch-In Leader

Hosts the evening Stitch-In for members who stitch after hours.

Interested in stepping into a role, or lending a hand at meetings? Email ngmprograms@needleworkguildmn.org and we’ll help you find a fit.
