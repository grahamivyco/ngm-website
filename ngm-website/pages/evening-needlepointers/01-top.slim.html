What we explore

Canvas work, after hours

An evening group for members who'd rather stitch after the workday. We dig into decorative stitches, specialty threads, and the finishing techniques that turn a canvas into a finished piece.

  • Specialty threads
  • Decorative stitches
  • Counted needlepoint
  • Finishing techniques
