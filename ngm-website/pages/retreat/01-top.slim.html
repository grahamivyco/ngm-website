About the retreat

More than a meeting

Once a year the Guild gathers for a retreat — a relaxed few days to take a class from a visiting teacher, dig into a project, and enjoy the company of fellow stitchers. It's one of the most anticipated events on the calendar.

Good to know

Common questions

Who can attend?
Members and their guests are warmly welcome.
Do I need to be experienced?
Not at all — classes span techniques and levels, and there's always open stitching.
How do I register?
Registration is through Wild Apricot — watch the calendar and the newsletter, or contact the Guild. Some classes fill quickly.
What are the kit fees?
Kit fees are set per class (about $75–$115) and are paid with registration. A few classes are lectures or demonstrations.
