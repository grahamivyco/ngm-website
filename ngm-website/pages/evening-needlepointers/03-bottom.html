What we explore

Canvas work, after hours

We meet the 3rd Thursday of each month, 7 to 9 pm — in person in Bloomington or by video. It's an evening group for members who'd rather stitch after the workday, and we dig into decorative stitches, specialty threads, and the finishing techniques that turn a canvas into a finished piece. Every experience level is welcome, whether you bring a canvas project or start something new with the group.

  • Specialty threads
  • Decorative stitches
  • Counted needlepoint
  • Finishing techniques

Good to know

Common questions

When and where does this group meet?
The 3rd Thursday of each month, 7 to 9 PM. It's hybrid — join in person in Bloomington or by video.
What will we work on?
Specialty threads, decorative stitches on canvas, counted needlepoint, and finishing techniques — or bring your own canvas project.
What skill level do I need?
Any. All experience levels are welcome.
How do I get the video link?
The videoconference link is on the Member Resources page — log in to access it. Trouble connecting? Email inquiry@needleworkguildmn.org.
