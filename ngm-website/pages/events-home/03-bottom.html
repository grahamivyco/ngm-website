Good to know

Event questions

Who can attend Guild events?
It depends on the event. The annual Retreat is members-only. Many workshops and programs are open to members, and some public events welcome everyone. Each event listing notes who can attend.
Do events cost extra beyond membership?
Most programs are free for members. Some workshops have a small materials fee for kits, and the Retreat has its own registration cost. Any fees are listed on the individual event page.
How do I register for an event?
Click any event above or on the calendar to open its page, where you can see the details and register. Members should log in first so member pricing and access apply.
Do I need to be a member to come to the Retreat?
Yes. The Retreat is for current members, and your membership must be active for the year the Retreat takes place. Joining is easy — it's $30 a year.
