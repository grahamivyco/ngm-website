What it is

A little bonus stitching

Some months have a fifth Tuesday — and when they do, the Guild adds an extra open-stitching session, so it comes around only a few times a year (watch the calendar). The venue is announced with each session, and there's no program, no agenda, and no signup — just drop in, bring whatever you're working on, and enjoy an afternoon of stitching and conversation.

  • No program
  • All levels
  • Just company
