Common questions

Good things to know

Can I attend a meeting without being a member?
You're welcome to visit and see if the guild is a good fit for you. Questions? Email membership@needleworkguildmn.org.
Do I have to participate in the meeting program?
Not at all. You're welcome to come and work on your own project — it doesn't even need to be related to the group's focus. Many members knit, crochet, or do other handwork while attending.
Do I need to be a member to attend the annual Retreat?
Yes. You must be a current member to register, and your membership must be active for the year in which the Retreat takes place.
