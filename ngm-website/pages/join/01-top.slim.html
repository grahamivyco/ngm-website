Good to know

Membership questions

Who can join the Guild?
Anyone with an interest in needlework is welcome — all skill levels, from curious beginners to lifelong stitchers. You don't need to be experienced to join.
How much are dues, and when do they renew?
Dues are $30 a year. The membership year runs June 1 through May 31, and renews annually.
Can I visit before I join?
Yes — your first visit to any meeting is always free. Come & Stitch and Sunday Tea & Stitch are open to everyone with no membership required.
How do I sign up?
Click "Join the Guild" to fill out a short membership form, set up your account, and pay your dues securely online. Questions? Email inquiry@needleworkguildmn.org.
