Lending library

Several hundred needlework books, kept in three locked cabinets at MVUUF and open during every meeting. Borrowing is free for current members — if the cabinets are locked, ask the meeting leader or a librarian to open them.

Checking out

How to borrow a book

Checkout happens at the cabinets during a meeting — there’s no online checkout. Four quick steps:

  1. 1

    Find the card

    It’s usually tucked inside the front or back cover of the book.

  2. 2

    Sign and date it

    Add your name and the date so the librarians know who has it.

  3. 3

    Drop the card in the pink box

    The pink box lives inside cabinet 1.

  4. 4

    Return it to the green box

    When you’re finished, place the materials in the green box in cabinet 1 and a librarian will process and reshelve them.

Good to know

Borrowing terms

One month

Materials go out for one month at a time.

Need it longer?

Check with a librarian — they’ll see whether anyone else is waiting for it.

Current members only

Borrowing is a benefit of current Guild membership.

Lost or unreturned

Materials that aren’t returned must be replaced or paid for.

Also in the cabinets

Archives & finding things

Archived materials

The Guild’s archived materials are stored in the same library cabinets. They’re available to use — just let a librarian know first.

Looking for something specific?

A refreshed catalog of the collection is in the works. Until then, the cabinets are open at every meeting — and the librarians are happy to help you track down a title or find something on a topic.
